Frequently Asked Questions

Let's clear up any little questions before your stay.

Can I store my luggage before check-in?
Yes, you can store your luggage before check-in. We accept it from 11:00 AM. We are also considering storing luggage for non-guests for a fee, so please see the Luggage Storage Service for details.
Can I stay with children or pets?
Preschool children can stay for free if they sleep with you. Unfortunately, pets are not allowed to stay with us. We are sorry.
Is there Wi-Fi? Can I use it for work?
Wi-Fi is available! You can use it for both work and personal use.
When does the cancellation fee apply?
There is no cancellation fee until 3 days before your stay. For cancellations after that or for no-shows without notice, we will charge 100% of the reservation fee.
