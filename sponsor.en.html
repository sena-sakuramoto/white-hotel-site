Why We Are Seeking Sponsors

We are a young architect team regenerating a 50-year-old inn as an "experiential storytelling hotel."

During the limited period from July to October 2025, we will conduct test operations while maintaining the current state, gathering guest feedback to inform our funding and design decisions as we aim to begin renovation construction in November.

We are seeking companies that resonate with this challenge and would like to partner with us in creating this new inn in Kamakura. We hope to weave the story of this inn together with all our sponsors.

Display Guidelines

About Sponsor Name Display

Based on city guidelines, third-party product/service promotion is not allowed. Only sponsor name display such as "sponsored by XX" is permitted.

About On-site Display

Outdoor displays will prioritize city standards and safety, featuring modest displays with sponsor names only. Details will be discussed individually.

Display Period

Displays will be during the limited operation period from July to October 2025. They will be temporarily suspended during the renovation period.

Sponsorship Content

We recommend content that resonates with the inn's values, such as corporate philosophy or community contribution activities.
